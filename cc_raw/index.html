About Pluto

Pluto is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper belt, discovered in 1930. Once considered the ninth planet of our solar system, Pluto is small but fascinating.

🌍 Size

Pluto is about 2,377 km in diameter — roughly two-thirds the size of Earth’s moon.

❄️ Temperature

Average surface temperature is -229°C, making it one of the coldest objects in our solar system.

🛰 Moons

Pluto has 5 known moons, with Charon being the largest and nearly half its size.

✨ Fun Facts about Pluto

  • Pluto takes 248 Earth years to complete one orbit around the Sun.
  • Pluto and Charon are sometimes called a "double planet".
  • A day on Pluto lasts about 6.4 Earth days.
